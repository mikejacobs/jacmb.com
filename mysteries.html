Every body is different. Our bodies can astound and betray us. Here are the things that remain mysteries about my personal existence in this world.

I often wake up sneezing.
Is it my allergy to dust mites?
I have some kind of blood sugar sensitivity.
If I go too long between meals, I can sometimes become hangry. I have learned to notice this and carry snacks. I've also noticed it is much more pronounced if I am coming down from a strong cup of coffee.
