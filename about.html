About

Last updated 09/08/2024

Tech world dropout turned garden slug. Reestablishing a relationship with time, place, and people. Reskilling in the Sonoran desert, harvesting rainwater to grow community foodscapes, collecting solar power. Sharing the abundance. Building dual power.

Lately, my focus has been on community projects away from the computer. But, when behind a keyboard, I'm often making music, art, code, or some combination of all three.

Exploring lately: optimistic visions of the future (social ecology, degrowth, usufruct, permaculture), food sovereignty, natural building, from-scratch cooking, crafts pushed aside in the descent to rampant consumerism, and figuring out how to carefully and minimually use technology in those scenarios.

I sometimes share things on Mastodon.
