Overview

The community center garden working group got approval from the community to build a greenhouse four months ago. Partway through building, another group realized there was an issue with the plan to entirely enclose a window on the building. Here are notes and design ides for making modifications.

Other Group Needs

  • light from the window
  • fire exit-ability
  • ventilation to fresh air when doing work with fumes
  • maintain appearance of security of metal bars

Garden Group Needs

  • to be able to have a greenhouse at the BCC
  • not have to entirely tear down, redesign, and rebuild the current greenhouse
  • not exposure to ventilated fumes on plants
  • positive to neutral vibes on the greenhouse from it's immediate neighbors at BCC
