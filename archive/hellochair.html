Hello, Chair Inc.

In 2007, I co-founded Hello Chair with two long-time friends from Tucson, AZ. Our goal was to apply cutting edge machine learning to solve a variety of problems in internet ad targeting and product recommendations—notably App Store discoverability. My responsibilites included: design, front-end development, accounting, marketing, hiring, managing, and many of the other odds and ends involved in running a small software startup. Hello Chair was lucky enough to have been backed by an amazing group of investors, including: Y-Combinator, Mitch Kapor, Chris Sacca and Harrison Metal.
